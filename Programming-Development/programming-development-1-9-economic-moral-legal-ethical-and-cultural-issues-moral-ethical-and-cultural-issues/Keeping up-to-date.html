Keeping up-to-date
Computer technology and the impact it has on our lives is ever-changing. It is therefore important that all students get into the habit of keeping up with these changes and reading widely. A student can learn facts and get a few ideas from websites such as ours, but they are no substitute for reading many articles about computing technology, different applications and the way we live changes as a result. This is not something that can be done at the last minute, a few weeks before exams! A students needs to get into the habit of reading three or four articles a week and making some notes about each of them. They should have a folder just for articles that have been read, printed off and notes made about so that when it come to exams, students should have many real examples to draw upon.
